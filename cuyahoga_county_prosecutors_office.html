Cuyahoga County Prosecutor's Office Timeline of the Arrest, Trial, and Execution of Cleveland Serial Killer Frank Spisak

The Justice Center, Cleveland, Ohio

Previous Next
Project Description
The client requested a timeline detailing the arrest, trial, and execution of Cleveland serial killer Frank Spisak taking place in the early 1980s. The timeline and was intended to inspire current and future attorneys.
Designed by
Dan Buckley
Constructed by
Vista Color Imaging - Cleveland, Ohio
My Role
Working directly with Assistant Prosecutor John Kosko, I was presented with photographs of the wall with a rough version of the timeline and boxes of physical and photographic evidence from the case. Starting with police sketches from the investigation as a background, I designed the timeline with the idea that I could add depth despite being a narrow hallway while still safely displaying the physical evididence from the case.
