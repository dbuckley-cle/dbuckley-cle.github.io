B'laster Corporation New Corporate Offices

Valley View, Ohio

Previous Next
Project Description
The client asked for help to design and produce decorative elements for the company's new corporate headquarters. The offices incorporated a rustic industrial theme.
Designed by
Dan Buckley
Constructed by
Vista Color Imaging - Cleveland, Ohio
My Role
Utilizing their branding, I designed three dimensional signage using brushed aluminum and plywood. I gave the logos a rustic look by digitally distressing the ink to give it the look of faded and peeling paint.
I also developed a theme for various wall coverings. Taking vintage public domain images including Lewis Hine's "Power house mechanic working on a steam pump" and others with an industrial theme, I placed images of the company's products to imply their use in the images.
