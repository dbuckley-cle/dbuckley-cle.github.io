Barbie - The Dreamhouse Experience

Sunrise, Florida - Berlin, Germany

Previous Next
Project Description
Two traveling entertainment venues creating full sized representations of the popular children's toy. The entire project was designed to be dismantled and relocated to various cities, one in North America and the other in Europe.
Designed by
EMS Entertainment - New York, New York
Constructed by
Gallo Displays with Marous Brothers Construction - Cleveland, Ohio
My Role
My involvement with EMS was to help provide artwork for various elements of the experience. The entire concept was to reproduce the feel of being inside a plastic doll house. Since many of the details in the toy version were represented by stickers, the designers wanted the same feel for the life size version. I created "stickers" that were used in various parts of the house including utensils for the kitchen counters, books for bookshelves, contents of the closet and even a sticker to represent the inside of a piano.
My involvement with Gallo displays was to prepare artwork for wallcovering throughout the project. Using architectural drawings as a guide I created artwork for printing patterns of wallpaper and brick textures to seamlessly fit the specific shape of each section of the traveling display.
