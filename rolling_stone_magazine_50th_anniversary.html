Rolling Stone Magazine 50th Anniversary Exhibit

Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and Museum - Cleveland, Ohio

Previous Next
Project Description
Traveling exhibit to commemorate the 50th anniversary of Rolling Stone Magazine.
Designed by
Pentagram - New York, New York
Constructed by
The Rogers Company - Mentor, Ohio
My Role
I worked closely with both the designers and builders of the exhibit to produce multi-panel wall and floor surfaces as well as dimensional exhibit elements such as prints to canvas, Tyvek, plexiglass, and wood.
Utilizing architectural drawings and renderings, I prepared files for print to large sheets of birch plywood which were then CNC routered to ensure precise seams for walls and flooring.
Other challenges included file prep for contour shaped plywood backgrounds, dye sublimated fabric lightboxes and vinyl wall covering.
